Step 1

Download the certificate

Click the Download Certificate button above. The file reelcast.pem will land in your Downloads folder.

Step 2

Open in Keychain Access

Double-click reelcast.pem in Finder. Keychain Access will open and prompt you to add it to your login keychain. Click Add.

Step 3

Set to Always Trust

In Keychain Access, find reelcast in the list (search for it). Double-click it to open the info window.

Expand the Trust section. Change When using this certificate to Always Trust. Close the window — macOS will ask for your password.

Step 4

Reload

Come back to this tab and click the button below. Safari should load without a warning.

Step 1

Download the certificate

Tap Download Certificate above. iOS will show a prompt — tap Allow.

Step 2

Install the profile

Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management. You'll see reelcast under Downloaded Profile. Tap it, then tap Install. Enter your passcode.

Step 3

Enable full trust

Go to Settings → General → About → Certificate Trust Settings. Find reelcast and toggle it on. Tap Continue on the warning.

Step 4

Reload

Return to Safari and reload. The warning should be gone.
